MY WRITING GUIDLINES

WRITE EVERY DAY
Walter Mosely talks about his main writing rule being “write every day”. This reminded me of what I wrote about productivity and discipline. Over time, small and consistent efforts add up to something large.

EDIT TO MUSIC
Read and edit, fun/angry and sad/beautiful chapters, to fun/angry and sad/beautiful music. Make playlists for editing. All songs must groove hard.

Fun/Angry – Static X. Crazy Town. Korn. Nine Inch Nails.
Sad/Beautiful – Kendrick Lamar. Korn. Dubstar.
