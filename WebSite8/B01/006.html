I like things that have a balance between intellect and passion.

Fiction that is thought-provoking without being pretentious.

Jazz that is catchy and grooves hard yet contains dynamic, rhythmic and harmonic complexity.

From time to time I also like to stand on one leg.

Lads – Part of being open-minded, and cultivating a balanced temperament, is being interested in many different personalities. In school my artistic friends encouraged me to value individuality, my nerdy friends challenged my prefrontal cortex and my hedonistic friends taught me how to party.


South Woodford Days – Med students, law students, engineering students, biology students. Halls of residence put a lot of different people from all over the place (Russia, Colombo, Norfolk, etc) together. Ice skating. Clubbing. Exploring the city. Good memories. Real people.
