On YT I recently discovered an interview with Sylvia Beach in 1962. I had never seen a video of her talking and it was great because I could compare what I was watching to Hemingway’s description of her in “A Moveable Feast”. The stories she told about the occupation of Paris, how it affected her and the bookshop, the liberation of Paris and Hemingway, were wonderful.
